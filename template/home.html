Overview

Finance ML is a multi module application implemented using the Machine learning techniques to demonstrate the use cases of ML in Finance domain like

  • Predicting whether a customer will be intrested in the term deposit or not
  • Customer Churn - Predicting whether a customer will leave the organization/bank
  • Home Loan Eligibility - whether the applicant is eligible for home loan or not
  • Signup for Loan - whether a customer will be intrested in personal Loan
  • Loan Default - whether the customer will default the loan

    • As you may already thinking, cool that will be a great piece of information for any finance organization to know about their customers and yes you are right.
